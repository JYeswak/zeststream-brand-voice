# LANGUAGE_BANK — mental-model-shifting phrases

Closes Grok framework item **M** (Mental Model Shifting Language Bank). These are the phrases that carry Meadows leverage — they shift SMB readers from "AI is overwhelming" to "AI is leverage I control after fixing foundations."

Use verbatim or adapt. Every phrase has a mental-model shift attached and a context.

---

## The gap (audience's current mental model)

> "AI is for enterprises / tech bros. It'll break what little works. I'm already behind."

## The target shift (post-ZestStream-copy)

> "Most systems can be fixed. One working build compounds. I'm not behind — I'm early."

---

## Tier 5 — anti-patterns to replace (use this as a find-and-replace)

| Banned phrase (or pattern) | Replace with |
|----------------------------|--------------|
| "Our enterprise-grade platform…" | "The stack I wire into yours…" |
| "Transform your operations" | "Wire the parts so the work lands" |
| "AI-powered solution" | "A workflow that uses [specific model / tool]" |
| "Unlock efficiency" | "Remove the handoff that's burning your Tuesdays" |
| "Synergize your data" | "Make your five data sources actually talk" |
| "Cutting-edge AI" | "Grok 4.1-fast at $0.20/M input" (name the thing) |
| "Streamline your workflow" | "Cut the manual copy-paste step" (name the step) |
| "Let us handle the complexity" | "I'll wire it. You review. You own what ships." |
| "Revolutionize your business" | "Ship one working build. See what compounds." |
| "Game-changing technology" | Cite the model. Cite the number. No adjectives. |

## Why this file exists (Meadows)

This is the **#2 leverage point** (paradigm) operationalized. Every phrase shifts a mental model. The reader's system (their beliefs about AI and their own situation) is the system being intervened on. Voice IS the intervention.

Not every phrase fits every page. **Rule:** one Tier 1 per top-level route, 2–3 Tier 2 per body section, Tier 3 on every CTA, Tier 4 only on /about and /work/*, Tier 5 applied globally as a grep.
